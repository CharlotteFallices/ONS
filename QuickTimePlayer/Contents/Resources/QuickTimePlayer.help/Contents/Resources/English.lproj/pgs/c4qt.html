Compression Options

Because uncompressed video requires a lot of disk space to store and a lot of bandwidth (the rate at which data can be transferred) to deliver, you'll want to compress (encode) your movie before sending it to another computer or on the web. Movies are usually compressed (encoded) as part of the export process.

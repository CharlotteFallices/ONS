QuickTime Player 7 Help

Simultaneously Playing Multiple Movies

You can have more than one QuickTime Player 7 window open at a time.

To open more than one QuickTime Player 7 window, do one of the following:

  • Double-click another movie file.
  • Choose View > Play All Movies.

By default, when you have more than one QuickTime Player 7 window open, the audio plays only on the active (frontmost) QuickTime Player 7 window. For information about changing this setting, see Changing Playback Preferences.
